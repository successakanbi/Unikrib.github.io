About unikrib

Unikrib is a student founded and focused platform, one that seeks to profer solutions to some of the challenges experienced by students while searching for apartments off campus, buying products and accessing services. We aim to solve this problems by providing location based connectivity between students and agents, vendors and service providers who may or may not be students.

Our vision

To render quality service to students in tertiary institutions in Nigeria and to become a leader in the student focused proptech space
